I'm Siddharth (Sid) Subramaniyam. I enjoy working in interdisciplinary fields and building large-scale systems.

Currently, I'm working on ML models for neural interfaces at Meta, establishing new HCI interfaces. Before that, I built retrieval models at Etsy to help people discover products they'd love through better search. I've also worked as a full-stack engineer, SRE, and ML researcher.

What really excites me is solving problems in interdisciplinary fields, where I get to learn from different experts: whether its training ML models, designing large-scale systems, analyzing genomic data. In grad school, I was fascinated by how cheap DNA sequencing was revealing the incredible variety of bacterial life around us. At the Ioerger Lab, I built computational tools (like Transit / ZINB) for statistical analysis of this data in order to learn more about these incredible beings.
